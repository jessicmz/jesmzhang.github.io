Projects

A compilation of person projects, work experience, and school projects

DJL’s core business goal is to make a game for a wide variety of audiences. Our game, Evasive Maneuvers, will fall under the “Bullet Hell” genre, which is characterized by overwhelming enemy waves and attack patterns. The game will feature a subtle color scheme and a minimalistic, 2D art style that follows the flat design trend. DJL’s strategic goals include visibility, simplicity, and familiarity. Our game will be completely free on the market, increasing visibility and therefore reaching as wide an audience as possible in the consumer market. The monetization strategy DJL will use is to provide an extensive, downloadable pack of game contents, including but not limited to: level and enemy variety, backgrounds, textures, weapons, and costumes that players can purchase for $2 to $5.

Molly is an eleven year old girl who has commissioned this project because she is concerned about losing her pet dog, Honey. Molly wants an app that will work on her cell phone. When she loses Honey, she will be able to turn on the app to locate Honey, and get a plan for finding her. The purpose of the system is to provide a solution to Molly’s problem.
